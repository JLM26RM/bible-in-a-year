# Bible in a Year — 261-Day Plan

A self-contained, phone- and computer-friendly checklist for reading through the
whole Bible in one year *minus 104 days* — so you can miss up to two days a week
and still finish on time. Daily mix of Old Testament, New Testament, and
Psalms/Proverbs, with ESV/LSB links, read-dates, a prayer list, and a start date.

Open the live app: see the GitHub Pages link in the repo's About section.
Your progress saves privately on your own device.
